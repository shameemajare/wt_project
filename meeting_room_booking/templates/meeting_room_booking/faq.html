FAQ

1. When can meeting rooms be booked?
-> Meeting rooms can be booked within our normal building opening hours.

2. What is the maximum number of people the meeting rooms hold?
-> It is the hirer’s responsibility to ensure that the appropriate size of room is selected and the specified room capacity is not exceeded. Our booking system will make the best sized room available to you.

3. Do the rooms have Wi-Fi?
-> All meeting rooms have password protected Wi-Fi. This is free to use with your meeting room booking – please ask if you would like to access the Wi-Fi and we can set you up with the password.

4. What other equipment is provided, or can be hired?
-> Room hire is just for the basic room, without any additional equipment. Various items are available to hire with your Meeting Room booking - please review and add these at the time of making your booking online.
